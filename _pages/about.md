## Who am I?

A PhD researcher in **Computer Science and Wireless Communication** with a deep passion for**Machine Learning, IoT, and Wireless Networks**. I am currently pursuing my doctoral studies at **Mohammed VI Polytechnic University (UM6P)**, Benguerir Campus, Morocco, where I work on Machine Learning for Energy Efficient IoT Networks. My work sits at the intersection of rigorous theoretical foundations and real-world engineering impact. I believe the most meaningful research emerges when we ask bold questions and translate academic insights into technology that genuinely matters.

---

## Research Focus

My doctoral research centers on **Energy Efficiency in IoT Network**. Specifically, I investigate **Homogeneous and Heterogeneous IoT Deployment in Flying Infrastructure**, with the goal of **Optimizing System-Wide Energy Efficiency in the Next Generation IoT**.

**Key research interests**:- Next-Generation IoT, Energy Efficiency, Low-Power Wide Area Network (LPWAN), UAV-assisted network, Advanced Machine Learning.

---

## Beyond Research

I am passionate about **AI Literacy, and Mentorship**. I believe that technology must be accessible to everyone, not just those with deep technical backgrounds. Whether through teaching, mentoring, or community initiatives, I am committed to empowering individuals with the knowledge, confidence, and skills needed to thrive in an AI-driven world and use technology responsibly to create meaningful impact.
